Fire Element Asanas

Virabhadrasana II (Warrior II)

Builds inner strength and courage, igniting inner fire.

Virabhadrasana III (Warrior III)

Enhances focus, balance, and core heat.

Utkatasana (Chair Pose)

Fires up the legs and stimulates metabolic activity.

Navasana (Boat Pose)

Activates the core and digestive fire (Agni).

Phalakasana (Plank Pose)

Ignites strength and fire in the entire body.
