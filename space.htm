Space Element Asanas

Padmasana (Lotus Pose)

Encourages silence, stillness, and space for inner awareness.

Savasana (Corpse Pose)

Promotes full relaxation and openness to spacious awareness.

Virasana (Hero Pose)

Helps open inner space while maintaining grounded posture.

Paschimottanasana

Creates space in spine and calms the nervous system.

Mandukasana (Frog Pose)

Opens inner hips and allows energy to flow through the space within.
