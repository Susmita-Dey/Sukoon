Earth Element Asanas

Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Promotes stability, grounding, and strength through stillness.

Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

Improves balance and connection to the ground like a tree.

Utkata Konasana (Goddess Pose)

Grounds energy while building strength in legs and hips.

Malasana (Garland Pose)

Opens the hips and connects to grounded energy in pelvis.

Prasarita Padottanasana

Helps release into earth energy while calming the mind.
